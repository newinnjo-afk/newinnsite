SUMMER OPEN HOURS (25th March onwards) Mon to Fri: 11am to 3pm and 6pm to Close Saturday: 11am to 3pm and 6pm to Close Sunday: 12am to 3pm and 6pm to Close

WINTER OPEN HOURS (1st January to 24th March ) Monday: 12pm to 2pm Tuesday to Friday: 11am to 2:30pm and 6pm to Close Saturday: 11am to 3pm and 6pm to Close Sunday: 12am to 3pm and 6pm to Close

PARKING We have a large private car park and drive entrance to the rear of the New Inn.

CAMPING AND MOTOR HOME STOP-OVERS There are two small fields for camping at the rear of the New Inn, Please book-in and pay before entering the camp site.

DISABLED, SENIORS AND CHILDREN We have done our best to make the New Inn wheel chair friendly. There is level parking and a covered walk way to the formal alfresco dining area and into the main bar area. Children are welcome in the bar but we ask that children are not in the bar after 20:30 in the evening.

FOOD ALLERGIES AND INTOLERANCES We are fully compliant with allergy regulations for all food and drinks. Special dietary requirements are available, please ask your server.
